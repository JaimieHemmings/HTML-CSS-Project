About Us

Tamar Aquatics is a family run business that has been established for over 30 years. We are experts in all things aquatic and have a wide range of products and services to suit all your needs.

Why Choose Us

Expert Staff

Our staff have a combined experience of over 50 years with some individual staff members having been working in the industry for over 10 years already. Many of our staff members even have OATA qualifications to back up what they say!

Quality Goods

All our staff are not only industry accredited experts but they are also head over fins for fish. We all have our own aquariums at home so you can rest assured that if we stock something, it's because we use it and recommend it!

Advice

Everything we stock has it's purpose but that doesn't mean it's good in every situation. If your dealing with an issue in your aquarium ranging from a minor algae growth to a sudden fish death, rest assured we can help you identify the issue and remedy it.
