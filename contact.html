FAQ

We frequently get asked the same questions repeatedly, in order to save you some time we've collated some of the more common ones and their answers below for your convenience. If we havn't covered your specific query please do still get in contact with us via the form below!

Algae is often a symptom of an imbalance in your aquarium. For this reason we usually recommend against using unnecassary chemicals to treat it. Instead we recommend you check your water parameters and make sure you are not overfeeding your fish. Finally, ensure your lights aren't too intense and ensure they are on for no longer than 6 hours a day. If you are still having issues, please contact us and we will be happy to help.

Fish are a great pet to have, but they do require some care. You will need to ensure you have a suitable tank for the species you want to keep, as well as a filter and heater (for tropical species). You will also need to ensure you are feeding them the correct food and that you are doing regular water changes. If you are unsure about anything, please contact us and we will be happy to help.

Fish can make a great gift, but we recommend you only do this if you are sure the person you are buying for is ready for the responsibility. Fish require care and attention and it is important that the person you are buying for is aware of this. If you are unsure, we recommend you buy them a gift card instead.
